Who are we

ECO Waste is a sustainable waste management company based in Rwanda. We utilize a cutting-edge web application to streamline and optimize waste management processes, striving to minimize environmental impact and promote a healthier ecosystem.

Our culture

At ECO Waste, we foster a culture of innovation, sustainability, and community. Our team is dedicated to finding eco-friendly solutions and continuously improving our processes to better serve our community and the environment.

How we work

Our approach combines advanced technology with sustainable practices. By leveraging our web application, we efficiently manage waste collection, processing, and disposal, ensuring minimal damage to the ecosystem. We prioritize transparency, efficiency, and environmental responsibility in all our operations.
